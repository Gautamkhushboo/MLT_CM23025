03

How Classification Works

①
Canvas Input: Your 280×280 drawing is downscaled to 28×28 pixels — the exact MNIST format.
②
Normalization: Each pixel value (0–255) is divided by 255 to get values in [0, 1].
③
CNN Forward Pass: The 28×28×1 tensor passes through Conv→Pool→Conv→Pool→Flatten→Dense layers.
④
Softmax Output: The final layer outputs 10 probabilities (one per digit). The highest is the prediction.
